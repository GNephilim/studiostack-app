## 🟢 Getting Started

### What is StudioStack?
StudioStack is a lesson scheduling and management app built for private music teachers. It helps you track students, book lessons, manage payments, and see reports — all in one place.

### How do I set up my profile?
Go to Settings and tap your name at the top. You can set your name, studio name, profile photo, and lesson rate. This information shows up on your dashboard and is used to calculate lesson costs.

### What's the difference between Free and Pro?
The free version lets you manage up to 10 students with basic scheduling and the upcoming lessons report. Pro unlocks unlimited students, all reports (income, cancellations, prepay, etc.), advanced settings like cancellation credit rules, auto-apply prepay, and more.

### How do I add my first student?
Go to the Students tab and tap the + button in the top right. Fill in their name, phone number, lesson frequency, and payment method. School name, age, and address are optional.

## 👥 Managing Students

### How do I edit a student's info?
Open a student from the Students tab, then tap the pencil icon in the top right. You can change their name, contact info, payment method, lesson frequency, billing mode, and custom rate.

### What does archiving a student do?
Archiving removes the student from your active list and deletes any future lessons. Completed and cancelled lessons are kept so your reports stay accurate. You can view and restore archived students anytime using the archive icon on the Students tab.

### What's the "Needs Attention" section?
This shows past lessons that haven't been marked as paid or completed. These are lessons whose scheduled time has passed but still need action — mark them done or cancel them to keep your records clean.

### How do I see a student's lesson history?
Open the student's detail page. The "Lesson History" link shows all completed lessons. Upcoming lessons are shown below as a compact summary grouped by month — tap any month to see individual lessons.

### Can I import contacts from my phone?
Yes! When adding a new student, tap "Import from Contacts" to pull in their name and phone number automatically.

### What does the lesson frequency setting do?
This is a label that describes how often the student typically takes lessons — Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Monthly, or One-Time. It's used for reporting and helps you remember each student's schedule pattern.

## 📅 Scheduling Lessons

### How do I book a lesson?
You can book from two places:
1. Open a student's detail and tap "Book Lesson"
2. On the Schedule tab, tap any open time slot on the day view

Pick a student, set the date, start time, duration, and optionally a lesson type.

### What are recurring lessons?
When booking, toggle "Recurring" to schedule the same lesson every week or every other week. You can set an end date (number of weeks) or choose "Indefinitely" which books out one year. Each recurring lesson is its own booking that you can manage individually.

### How do I switch between day, week, and month views?
Use the view toggle at the top of the Schedule tab — Day, Week, Month, or List. The Day view shows a full timeline with your lessons. Use the arrows or "Today" button to navigate between dates.

### What do the grey "Outside Availability" slots mean?
These are hours outside your set availability (configured in Settings > Availability). They're shown so you can still see the full day, but they're dimmed to indicate you normally don't teach during those times.

### How do I cancel a lesson?
Open the lesson from the schedule or student detail, then tap "Cancel Lesson." Pick a reason (Student Cancelled, No-Show, Weather, etc.). The lesson stays in your records for reporting — it's not deleted.

### Can I edit a lesson after booking it?
Yes, as long as it hasn't been completed or cancelled. Open the lesson and tap the pencil icon. You can change the date, time, duration, and notes.

## 💰 Payments & Billing

### How do I mark a lesson as paid?
On any lesson row, tap the dollar sign icon to toggle paid status. You can also mark it from the lesson detail page.

### What happens when I tap "Done" on a lesson?
If the lesson is already marked as paid, it completes instantly. If not paid, you'll see a confirmation with two options: "Mark Paid & Complete" (recommended) or "Complete Without Payment." This prevents accidentally losing income in your reports.

### What is Monthly Prepay?
Some students pay a lump sum at the beginning of the month to cover all their lessons. Set a student's billing mode to "Monthly Prepay" in their edit page. Then record payments through their Prepay Summary card. The system tracks their balance, credits from cancellations, and carry-forward amounts.

### How does auto-apply prepay work?
When enabled in Settings > Lesson Pricing, completing a prepay student's lesson automatically marks it as paid and deducts from their balance. If the balance runs low, you'll get an alert before confirming. This saves you from tapping both "Paid" and "Done" on every lesson.

### What if a prepay student's balance runs out?
You'll see a "Prepay balance is low" alert when trying to complete a lesson. You can still mark it paid anyway or complete without payment. The student's detail page will show a negative balance, so you'll know they owe for next month.

### What's the difference between Per-Lesson and Prepay billing?
**Per-Lesson:** You mark each lesson as paid individually. The student pays after or at each lesson.

**Prepay:** The student pays upfront for the month, and lessons are deducted from their balance as they're completed.

## 📊 Reports

### What reports are available?
- **Upcoming Lessons** *(Free)* — shows scheduled lessons by date range
- **Students by Frequency** *(Free)* — breaks down your students by how often they take lessons
- **Income Report** *(Pro)* — earnings by date range with student drill-down
- **Lessons by Type** *(Pro)* — see which lesson categories are most popular
- **Open Time Slots** *(Pro)* — find gaps in your schedule and book directly
- **Prepay & Credits** *(Pro)* — track prepay balances and credit history
- **Cancelled Lessons** *(Pro)* — breakdown by reason and student

### How does the Income Report work?
It shows your earnings from completed, paid lessons over a selected date range. Tap on any student to see their individual contribution. You can switch between a summary view and breakdown by student.

### What does the Cancelled Lessons report show?
A breakdown of all cancelled lessons by reason (Student Cancelled, No-Show, Weather, etc.) and by student. Tap a reason to expand and see individual cancelled lessons. It also shows lost revenue and how many cancellations earned the student a credit.

### Can I share or export a report?
Each report has a Share button at the bottom that generates a text summary you can send via Messages, Email, or any other app.

### What is the Open Time Slots report?
It finds gaps in your schedule where you could book new lessons, based on your availability settings. Tap any open slot to go directly to the booking screen with that time pre-filled.

## 🔄 Cancellations & Credits

### What happens when I cancel a lesson?
The lesson stays in your records as "Cancelled" with the reason you selected. It's not deleted, so your reporting history stays complete. Cancelled lessons don't count toward income.

### Which cancellation reasons earn a credit?
By default:
- ✅ **Holiday** — earns credit
- ✅ **Teacher Unavailable** — earns credit
- ✅ **Weather** — earns credit
- ❌ **Student Cancelled** — no credit (student forfeits)
- ❌ **No-Show** — no credit (student forfeits)

You can customize this in Settings > Cancellation Policy (Pro).

### Can I change which reasons earn credit?
Yes, in Settings > Cancellation Policy (Pro feature). Toggle each cancellation reason on or off for credit. For example, you could make Weather a non-credit reason if you prefer.

## ⚙️ Settings & Configuration

### How do I set my available hours?
Go to Settings > Availability. Choose which days you teach, then optionally add specific time windows for each day (e.g., Monday 3:00 PM – 8:00 PM). Your schedule view will show hours outside your availability as dimmed.

### How do I set my lesson rate?
Go to Settings > Lesson Pricing. Enter your rate and choose whether it's per hour or per lesson. Individual students can have custom rates set in their edit page, which overrides the global rate.

### What are Lesson Types?
Lesson Types let you categorize lessons (e.g., Drums, Piano, Guitar, Theory). Go to Settings > Lesson Types to create and manage them. Each type gets a custom color and icon. When booking a lesson, you can optionally assign a type.

### How do notifications work?
Go to Settings > Notifications to enable lesson reminders. Choose how many minutes before a lesson you'd like to be notified (5, 10, 15, 30, or 60 minutes). Notifications only fire for lessons where the student has reminders enabled.

### How do I back up my data?
Go to Settings > Backup & Restore. Tap "Create Backup" to save all your data as a file. You can restore from a backup at any time. You can also export your lesson schedule to a calendar file.

### How do I change the app's appearance?
Go to Settings > Appearance. Choose between Light, Dark, or System (matches your device setting).

## ⭐ StudioStack Pro

### What do I get with Pro?
- **Unlimited students** (free is limited to 10)
- **All reports:** Income, Lessons by Type, Open Time Slots, Prepay & Credits, Cancelled Lessons
- **Cancellation credit customization** — choose which reasons earn credits
- **Auto-apply prepay** — one-tap lesson completion for prepay students
- **Priority support**

### How do I upgrade to Pro?
Go to Settings > StudioStack Pro and tap the upgrade button. It's a one-time purchase through the App Store — no subscription.

### Can I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?
Yes! Go to Settings > StudioStack Pro and tap "Restore Purchase." Your Pro status is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.

### What if I hit the 10-student limit?
On the free plan, you can manage up to 10 active students. Archived students don't count toward this limit. If you need more than 10 active students, upgrade to Pro for unlimited.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### I'm not getting lesson reminders
Make sure notifications are enabled in Settings > Notifications, and that the specific student has reminders turned on. Also check that you've allowed StudioStack notifications in your iPhone's Settings > Notifications > StudioStack.

### My lesson costs show $0.00
You may not have set a lesson rate yet. Go to Settings > Lesson Pricing and enter your rate. If a student has a custom rate, make sure it's greater than $0. Lesson costs are calculated when the lesson is booked — existing lessons won't update automatically.

### A student's balance seems wrong
Prepay balances are calculated from all recorded payments minus delivered lesson costs and forfeitures. Check that:
1. Monthly payments are recorded correctly
2. Completed lessons are marked as paid
3. Cancellation credit settings match your policy

### I accidentally completed a lesson without marking it paid
Once a lesson is marked complete, it's locked — but you can still update the payment status. Tap the lesson to open its detail, then tap the "Unpaid" icon in the status card. A confirmation will ask if you'd like to mark it as paid. This updates your income reports without unlocking the lesson.
