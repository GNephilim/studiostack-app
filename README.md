# 🎵 StudioStack

**The all-in-one lesson management app for private music teachers.**

StudioStack helps you manage your students, schedule lessons, track payments, and generate reports — all from your iPhone. Whether you teach 5 students or 50, StudioStack keeps your studio organized so you can focus on what matters: teaching.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📋 Student Management
- Add and manage your full student roster
- Import contacts directly from your phone
- Track lesson frequency (Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Monthly, One-Time)
- Set per-student custom lesson rates
- Archive and restore students without losing history
- Flexible billing modes: Per-Lesson or Monthly Prepay

### 📅 Scheduling
- **Day, Week, Month, and List views** for your lesson calendar
- Book single or **recurring lessons** (weekly, bi-weekly)
- Set your **available hours** — unavailable times are dimmed on the schedule
- **Tap any open time slot** to instantly book a lesson
- Cancel lessons with tracked reasons (Student Cancelled, No-Show, Weather, Holiday, etc.)
- Edit lessons anytime before completion
- **"Today" button** to quickly jump back to the current day

### 💰 Payments & Billing
- **Mark lessons as Paid or Done** with one tap
- Smart completion flow — warns you if a lesson hasn't been marked as paid before completing
- **Monthly Prepay** billing mode with automatic balance tracking
- **Auto-Apply Prepay** (Pro) — completing a prepay student's lesson automatically deducts from their balance
- Low balance alerts when prepay funds are running out
- Fix missed payments — mark completed lessons as paid even after they're locked

### 📊 Reports
- **Upcoming Lessons** — see what's on your schedule *(Free)*
- **Students by Frequency** — breakdown by lesson frequency *(Free)*
- **Income Report** — earnings by date range with student drill-down *(Pro)*
- **Lessons by Type** — see which lesson categories are most popular *(Pro)*
- **Open Time Slots** — find gaps in your schedule and book directly *(Pro)*
- **Prepay & Credits** — track prepay balances and credit history *(Pro)*
- **Cancelled Lessons** — breakdown by reason and student with lost revenue tracking *(Pro)*
- Share any report via Messages, Email, or AirDrop

### 🔔 Notifications
- Lesson reminders: 5, 10, 15, 30, or 60 minutes before a lesson
- Per-student notification control
- Works with iOS native notification system

### ⚙️ Settings & Customization
- **Teacher Profile** — name, studio name, photo, contact info
- **Lesson Pricing** — set your rate per hour or per lesson
- **Lesson Types** — create custom categories with colors and icons (Drums, Piano, Guitar, Theory, etc.)
- **Availability** — set your teaching hours per day of the week
- **Cancellation Policy** (Pro) — choose which cancellation reasons earn student credits
- **Backup & Restore** — full data backup and calendar export
- **Appearance** — Light, Dark, or System theme

### 🛡️ Data Privacy
- **All data stored locally on your device** — nothing is uploaded to any server
- No analytics, no tracking, no ads
- In-app purchases handled securely through Apple's StoreKit
- [View our Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md)
