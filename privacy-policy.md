# StudioStack Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2025

## Overview
StudioStack is a lesson scheduling app. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection
StudioStack does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All student information, lesson records, and app data are stored **locally on your device** using Apple's Core Data framework.

## Third-Party Services
- **Apple StoreKit** is used to process in-app purchases. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
- StudioStack does **not** use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

## Data Storage
All data remains on your device (and your private iCloud account if iCloud sync is enabled). We have no access to your data.

## Children's Privacy
StudioStack is not directed at children under 13. Student records entered by the user are stored locally and never transmitted.
